Bilkent University's Graphic Design Senior Exhibition gives its final year students a chance to ease into their professional lives. The independent comic project WonderKids will be unveiled at the event.

Project Participants

Sageda Alfadil

Sageda is a fourth year Graphic Design student with an interest in illustrative narrative and conceptual development. She is the creator of WonderKids.

Erhan Tunalı

Erhan Tunalı is the Acting Chair at Bilkent University's Graphic Design department. He has played a big role in the making of WonderKids through his supervision.

Efe Alpay

Efe Alpay is an Assistant Professor at Bilkent University's Graphic Design department. His input aided in the making of WonderKids.

Marek Brzozowski

Marek Brzozowski studied in the Academy of Fine Art in Gdańsk, Poland. He is a mentor for WonderKids.
